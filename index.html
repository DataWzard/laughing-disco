Get to know me!

I'm a Solutions Engineer with a strong foundation in back-end development, cloud infrastructure, and statistical modeling. I enjoy tackling messy, ambiguous problems and translating them into clear, actionable insights. I specialize in turning raw data into operational tools that drive real impact.


My work spans the full data lifecycle; from building ETL pipelines and automating workflows to deploying machine learning models and designing dashboards. I thrive at the intersection of engineering and analysis, where performance matters and clarity drives decisions.
